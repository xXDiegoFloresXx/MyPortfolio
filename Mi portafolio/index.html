Portfolio

In this section, you’ll find some of the projects I've worked on during my studies and internships. From mechanical design to control systems and embedded software, these projects showcase my diverse skill set and passion for engineering.

FIRST Robotics Competition 2019

Designed and built a competitive robot for the 2019 FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). As part of a highly motivated team, I played a crucial role in the mechanical design and CAD modeling of the robot, while also considering the integration of electronic components for effective cable management and layout planning. My responsibilities included developing design concepts, collaborating with teammates on CAD work, and ensuring that our designs accommodated the necessary sensors, actuators, and wiring requirements. Additionally, I contributed to the assembly and testing phases, gaining valuable hands-on experience in iterative design, teamwork, and engineering problem-solving.

FIRST Robotics Competition 2020

Participated in the 2020 edition of the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC), where we focused on refining our mechanical design and CAD modeling skills to create a robot capable of handling complex autonomous and teleoperated tasks. I contributed to the team by working on the CAD design, collaborating with teammates to optimize the robot’s structure and layout, and ensuring proper cable management for reliable performance. In addition to my design responsibilities, I also served as the primary driver of the robot during the competition, helping to implement our strategies on the field and ensuring smooth operation during matches.

John Deere's fixture

Collaborated with John Deere on an engineering project focused on designing and building a custom fixture specifically for bending metal plates. Throughout this project, I worked closely with engineers and manufacturing specialists to ensure the fixture met stringent performance and quality standards. This experience significantly enhanced my understanding of precision manufacturing processes, including tolerances, material behaviors during bending, and the importance of robust fixture design for repeatable, high-quality outcomes. Additionally, I gained valuable insights into collaborative engineering practices and how to integrate customer requirements into practical, effective solutions.
